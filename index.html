About Us

Fleego is a next-gen fleet management service focused on Uber cab operations. We provide drivers with structured shifts, higher earnings, free food and accommodation, and 24/7 support. Our mission is to create a reliable, scalable, and efficient urban mobility network for both drivers and passengers.
